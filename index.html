Fast facts

The MINGLE survey integrates ALMA observations with complementary instruments to produce calibrated images, catalogs, and spectroscopic cubes. We release curated, science-ready data products and supporting documentation for community use.

  • Data releases: periodic public releases
  • Products: images, catalogs, spectra
  • Access: direct downloads and programmatic metadata
