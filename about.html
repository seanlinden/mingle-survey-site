About the MINGLE Survey

The MINGLE Survey (Multi-Instrument Next-Generation Legacy Exploration) is focused on producing community-ready datasets by combining ALMA observations with complementary facilities. Our goals:

  • Deliver calibrated, science-ready images and catalogs
  • Publish reproducible data reduction methods
  • Support open access and community-driven science
